### P-ML12a — Cross-Asset Comovement Research (F19) ✅

Created `data/cross_asset.py` (yfinance fetcher for SPY, QQQ, GLD, TLT, UUP, ^VIX)
and comprehensive research notebook `notebooks/p_ml12a_cross_asset_analysis.ipynb`.

**Dataset:** 6 traditional assets aligned with BTC daily data (2019-2025).
~1510 business days, ~28% BTC bars dropped from weekend alignment.

**Key findings:**

#### 1. Correlation Structure by Era
- **Era 4 (Bear, 2022-2024):** Strongest BTC-SPY correlation (+0.453***), BTC-QQQ (+0.471***).
  BTC traded like a levered tech stock during Fed tightening.
- **Era 5 (ETF, 2024+):** BTC-SPY correlation remains strong (+0.363***) but slightly lower.
  BTC spot ETF creates a direct TradFi-crypto link.
- BTC-UUP (USD) is significantly negative in Era 4 (−0.274***) — dollar strength hurts BTC.
- BTC-VIX is strongly negative (−0.380*** in Era 4, −0.317*** in Era 5) — BTC is a risk asset.

#### 2. Asymmetric Tail Dependence (most important finding)
- **Left tail (BTC worst 10% of days):** BTC-SPY correlation = +0.504
- **Middle 80%:** BTC-SPY correlation = +0.339
- **Right tail (BTC best 10%):** BTC-SPY correlation = +0.005
- **Implication:** Correlations spike during crises but disappear during rallies.
  Cross-asset features are most useful as *downside risk* indicators.

#### 3. VIX-Conditional Correlation
- VIX Low [12-14]: BTC-SPY corr = +0.061 (essentially zero)
- VIX High [23-39]: BTC-SPY corr = +0.547 (very strong)
- This confirms VIX level as a powerful *regime context* feature for the model.

#### 4. Lead-Lag Relationships
- No strong 1-5 day lead from equities to BTC. Contemporaneous (lag=0) dominates.
- After SPY drop >2%: BTC t+3 = −2.69%, t+5 = −4.62% (vs unconditional +0.30%, +0.53%).
  This is a *concurrent/trailing* effect, not predictive.

#### 5. Volume Cross-Analysis
- High-volume SPY days show much higher BTC-SPY correlation (Q5: +0.579 vs Q1: +0.318).
- BTC-SPY volume correlation = +0.304 (Era 4), +0.347 (Era 5) — growing interconnection.

#### 6. Regime-Conditional Correlation
- **Bear regime:** BTC-SPY = +0.577 (strongest — institutional selling is synchronized)
- **Bull regime:** BTC-SPY = +0.151 (weak — crypto rallies are more idiosyncratic)
- **Ranging:** BTC-SPY = +0.467 (moderate)

#### 7. Fold 2 Deep Dive
- Nov 2021 ATH decline: BTC peaked Nov 2021, SPY peaked Jan 2022. BTC *led* equities —
  cross-asset features would have been lagging, not leading.
- Note: Era 1-3 data alignment issue (timezone handling) limited some early-era analysis.
  Core insights come from Era 4-5 where institutional link is strongest and most relevant.
