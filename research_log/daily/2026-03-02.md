`vol_log_chg` and `di_diff` are the clearest sign-flippers. → **F9 logged.**

### P-ML4 — Regime-specific LightGBM ensemble (F10) ✅

Built `ml/models/ensemble.py`:

```python
class RegimeEnsemble:
    """Train separate LGBMForecasters for bull and non-bull bars.

    Fallback: if < min_bull_bars (30) bull training bars exist,
    sign-flip the non-bull model for bull test bars (P-ML3 Exp-B).
    """
```

Training-fold regime distribution (key constraint):

| Fold | Bull bars | Bear bars | Ranging bars | Bull model? |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 0 | 89 | 89 | FALLBACK |
| 2 | 1 | 120 | 146 | FALLBACK |
| 3 | 128 | 35 | 104 | YES |
| 4 | 133 | 30 | 104 | YES |
| 5 | 136 | 1 | 130 | YES |

Notebook `notebooks/ml_regime_specific_models.ipynb` evaluates the ensemble per-fold:

| Fold | Non-bull IC | Bull IC | P-ML4 IC |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | −0.066 | +1.00† | −0.051 |
| 2 | −0.017 | −0.162 | −0.053 |
| 3 | −0.131 | **−0.138** | −0.148 |
| 4 | −0.087 | **−0.063** | −0.056 |
| 5 | +0.042 | **−0.044** | −0.003 |

†Fold 1 bull: 2 test bars, degenerate.

**Bull model IC stays negative in all fitted folds** — the model learns mean-reversion
patterns, not trend continuation. 128 daily bull bars at 1-day horizon is insufficient.

**Equity:** Baseline −30.2% → P-ML4 −2.5% (Sharpe +0.227) → P-ML3 Exp-C +8.8% (Sharpe +0.280)

Exp-C (regime-gated, no separate model) still outperforms. P-ML4 improved over baseline but
could not surpass Exp-C. Root cause: insufficient bull training data and short label horizon.

**Next options (not yet scheduled):**
- Extend dataset to 2019 (6yr total) for more bull training bars
- Use 3–5d forward return horizon for bull model (trend needs more time to develop)

→ **F10 logged.**
